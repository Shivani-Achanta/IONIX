ABOUT US


Welcome to IONIX, where innovation meets intensity!
Presented by the Electrical and Electronics Department at Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology and Management.


Brace yourself for an electrifying experience like no other.
This 24-hour build challenge transcends the typical hackathon experience and is proudly presented by the technical club, NATEECrats.


In collaboration with IEEE, we’ve created a national-level platform where participants from colleges across the country converge to tackle real-world problems head-on.


At IONIX, creativity sparks, ideas ignite, and solutions take shape.
Our participants dive into the realms of IoT, Embedded Systems, and Robotics, fueled by passion and guided by expertise.


As the clock ticks, projects evolve, and innovation thrives.
The best part?
Our judges, seasoned professionals from renowned start-ups, evaluate the top-quality projects.


IONIX isn’t just an event; it’s a journey.
Join us as we redefine limits, break barriers, and celebrate the spirit of technology.
The maiden event in 2024 set the stage, and now it’s your turn to be part of the revolution
